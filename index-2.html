ABOUT US

AHRAM SCAN is a highly prestigious and advanced Radiology center in Egypt providing round the clock services for patients. Backed by a team of highly qualified radiologists, trained professionals and paramedical staff, AHRAM SCAN offers a wide range of services for the diagnosis & treatment of both vascular and non-vascular procedures.
